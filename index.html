OUR STORY

Since 1984

Born in the golden era of Pacific Beach, Cass Street Bar & Grill has been the neighborhood's living room for over four decades.

What started as a simple bar three blocks from the sand has become a PB institution. We're the kind of place where surfers, locals, and visitors all feel at home — where the baguettes are legendary, the beers are always cold, and the vibes are unmatched.

Step inside and you'll find 80s-inspired synthwave art on the walls, neon glowing off classic car murals, pool tables waiting for challengers, and 24 taps of the best craft beers San Diego has to offer.

QUESTIONS?

Got Questions?

What are your hours? +

Monday through Thursday: 11am to 10pm. Friday and Saturday: 11am to 2am. Sunday: 11am to 10pm. We're open 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

Do you take reservations? +

We don't take reservations — we're a casual, walk-in kind of place! First come, first served. There's always room at the bar.

Is there parking nearby? +

Street parking is available on Cass Street and surrounding blocks. There's also a public lot nearby. Pro tip: walk or bike — you're in PB!

When is happy hour? +

Happy hour is every day from 3pm to 6pm with $5 pints, $5 wine glasses, and $5 select cans. Day drinking specials run from open until 6pm too.

Do you offer delivery or takeout? +

Yes! You can order for pickup through our website, or get delivery through DoorDash. But honestly, the vibes are better in person.

Is the bar wheelchair accessible? +

Yes, Cass Street Bar & Grill is wheelchair accessible. Everyone's welcome here.
